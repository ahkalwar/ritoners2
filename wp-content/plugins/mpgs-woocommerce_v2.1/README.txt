### INSTALL MPGS WOOCOMMERCE PAYMENT GATEWAY
1. Upload the files to your Plugin directory.
2. Sign in to your Wordpress admin.
3. Click **Woocommerce Setting** tab and **Checkout Tab**
4. Click **MPGS Pro** to config plguin  .
5. Enter your **Checkout URL:** (GIVE FROM YOUR BANK). Exam test:https://eu-gateway.mastercard.com/checkout/version/43/checkout.js OR https://paymentgateway.commbank.com.au/checkout/version/40/checkout.js 
7. Enter your **Merchant ID:**. (GIVE FROM YOUR BANK) 
8. Enter your **API password:**. (GET FROM OPERATOR LOGIN) 
9. Enter your **Merchant Name:**. The text display in the page gateway
10. Enter your **Address:**. The text display in the page gateway
